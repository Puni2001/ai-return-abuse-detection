    Frequently Asked Questions

    How is this different from existing solutions?

    Existing systems react after losses occur using static rules. Our AI predicts risk proactively and adapts to patterns, differentiating genuine customers from abusers.

    Will this hurt genuine customers?

    No! We use graduated actions - low-risk customers get instant refunds. Only high-risk cases require additional verification. This protects both business and customer experience.

    What makes it India-specific?

    We incorporate COD payment patterns, festival season behaviors, regional variations, and local market dynamics unique to the Indian e-commerce landscape.

    How accurate is the AI model?

    Current rule-based model provides consistent risk scoring. ML model (in development) targets >85% accuracy with continuous learning from real data.

    Can I integrate this with my platform?

    Yes! Simple REST API integration. Send order details, receive risk score and recommended action in milliseconds.
